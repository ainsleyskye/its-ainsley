SafariZone Digital Pokemon Department Store

When Ainsley was placed with Kendal, Joe, & Dom for their capstone project, the group was only told that the project must be a mock-up of an e-commerce site and given the basic project requirements. Luckily, everyone in our group is a huge fan of the Pokemon games! So we decided to answer a question: if the people in the Pokemon games have such advanced technology to capture wild beasts in tiny little PokeBalls, why do Pokemon trainers have to visit physical shops to buy items that they may need on their quests? Thus, the SafariZone Department Store was born, a one-stop-shop right at your fingertips for any and all items a Pokemon trainer may need! Buy Some PokeBalls!
